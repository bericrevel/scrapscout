## Description (4000 chars max)

ScrapScout — The AI Salvage Intelligence Network

Turn trash into cash. ScrapScout uses advanced AI vision to instantly identify scrap materials, estimate real-time market values, and surface the highest-yield salvage opportunities near you.

SCAN ANYTHING
Point your camera at any item and get an immediate AI assessment: material, condition, brand, resale value, and scrap value. Copper pipe, aluminum cans, vintage electronics, furniture — if it has value, ScrapScout finds it.

OPPORTUNITY MAP
See estate sales, bulk trash pickups, government auctions, and free item listings mapped in real time around you. Filter by legal status. Founder members get 2-hour early access to high-value events.

MARKET WATCH
Live scrap metal prices for copper, aluminum, brass, and steel. AI-powered Hold or Sell signals based on real market data. Know the right moment to sell.

AI NEGOTIATOR
Get a word-for-word negotiation script built around the exact item in front of you. Walk into every deal knowing your number, your leverage, and your floor.

LISTING GENERATOR
One tap writes a complete optimized listing for Facebook Marketplace, Craigslist, or eBay. Title, description, price strategy, and tags — all done.

INVENTORY TRACKER
Log every find with AI-generated details. Track status from found to sold. Full cloud sync across devices.

SCOUT-AI CHAT
Ask anything. Salvage strategy, material identification, scrap yard recommendations, negotiation advice — Scout-AI has you covered.
