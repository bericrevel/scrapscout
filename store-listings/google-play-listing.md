## Full Description (4000 chars max)

ScrapScout — The AI Salvage Intelligence Network

Turn trash into cash with the most advanced salvage scouting app ever built. ScrapScout uses cutting-edge AI vision to identify scrap materials, estimate real-time market values, and guide you to the highest-yield opportunities in your area.

AI Material Scanner
Point your camera at any item and ScrapScout's AI instantly identifies the material, condition, brand, and market value. Copper, aluminum, brass, steel, electronics, antiques — if it has value, ScrapScout finds it. Get both resale and scrap value estimates so you always know your best exit.

Opportunity Map
Real-time map of estate sales, bulk trash pickups, auctions, and free listings near you. Filter by legal status so you always stay above board. Founder members get 2-hour early access to high-value events before anyone else.

Inventory Tracker
Log everything you find with photos, condition notes, and value estimates. Track your haul from found to sold. Your inventory syncs to the cloud so it is always with you.

Live Market Watch
Real-time scrap metal prices for copper, aluminum, brass, and steel. AI-powered Hold or Sell recommendations based on current market trends. Know exactly when to move your material.

AI Negotiator
Get an AI-generated negotiation script tailored to the specific item you are buying or selling. Know your counter-offer, your leverage, and your walk-away number before you open your mouth.

Restoration Guide
For items worth fixing up, get a step-by-step restoration guide with required tools, safety tips, and pro secrets to maximize the final resale value.

Listing Generator
One tap generates a fully optimized listing for Facebook Marketplace, Craigslist, or eBay — complete with SEO title, compelling description, pricing strategy, and tags.

Temporal Log
Track bulk trash pickup schedules, estate sale dates, and auction days. Never miss a high-value window again.

Scout-AI Chat
Ask Scout-AI anything about salvage, scrap prices, identification, negotiation, or market trends. Get expert-level answers in seconds.
