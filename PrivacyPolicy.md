# Privacy Policy for ScrapScout

**Effective Date:** March 19, 2026

ScrapScout ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and disclose your information when you use our mobile application.

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1. Location Data
ScrapScout requires access to your device's precise location (GPS) to provide real-time mapping of salvage opportunities and navigation. This data is processed on your device and sent to our servers only to fetch nearby opportunities.

### 1.2. Camera Data
We require access to your device's camera to analyze salvage items using AI. Images captured are sent to the Gemini API for analysis and are not stored permanently on our servers unless you explicitly save them to your inventory.

### 1.3. User Account Information
If you create an account or subscribe, we collect your email address and payment information (processed securely via Stripe or Web2wave).

## 2. How We Use Your Information
- To provide and maintain our Service.
- To notify you about changes to our Service.
- To provide customer support.
- To gather analysis or valuable information so that we can improve our Service.

## 3. Third-Party Services
We use the following third-party services:
- **Google Gemini API**: For image and data analysis.
- **Stripe / Web2wave**: For payment processing.
- **Leaflet**: For map rendering.

## 4. Security
The security of your data is important to us, but remember that no method of transmission over the Internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure.
